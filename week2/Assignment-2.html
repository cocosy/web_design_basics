Details

What is the purpose of the website?

The purpose of the website is to show my design works online. But I also want people to know about me in life. I’d like people to know me as a designer, also as a traveler.

Who is the intended audience?

The intended audience will be both my peers and my potential employers who want to see my design works and know more about me and my life.

What tone do you want for the site’s written content?

There won’t be quite a lot written content on my site. For descriptions about my works, I prefer them to be more formal. But for the rest part of my traveler’s notes, I’d prefer to use a less formal tone.

What will be the primary content of your site?

I want my landing page to have a little interactivity. I made a quick draft of the landing page. Basically I want to achieve basic interactive of the door of the house open when click to enter.

Then maybe the five different categories of the navigation bar become five different doors.

Or more ideally, it will be something like this website. I want to build the website like an architecture. So when user scroll down, a different room comes up. It's like each part of the website is an individual room!

What will be the secondary content?

And then in each room or door is my works and all kinds of stuffs laying out in a simple and clean way.
