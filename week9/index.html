The general introduction to my new site:

I want to build a new blog kind website as a branch of my portfolio website. The main purpose of the website is about my life, my traveling and may work as my diary as well. I will remove the current traveling page from my portfolio site into my new blog site. My blog website will be a diverse collection and more personal.

Who is the intended audience?

The intended audience will be my close friends and mostly me.

What tone do you want for the site’s written content?

There won’t be quite a lot written content on my site. I will use a more casual tone.

What will be goal and content of your site?

Here are several goals I’d like to achieve in my blog website:

  • I want to have music embedded into my website.
  • I want to make one page as my (partly visual narrative) diary. And create it to be interactive in a way.(possibly work with Wordpress)
  • I want to build a page for movies/music/books that I read. Just want to archive them in a way. It might be simply a picture of them, like a picture wall. I have find a sample.
  • I want to have a section that has a user feedback page; so people can talk to me. Or something like shop that people can order something from me. Or both! (haven’t decided yet)
  • And as a DT student, I want to put some of my Processing sketch into my portfolio site if possible. So people can interact with them.
